Travel to North Korea

Places you can visit:

  • Pyongyang: The capital city.
  • Kaesong: Near the DMZ.
  • Mount Paektu: A sacred volcano.

Traveler Tips

  1. Always stay with your official tour guides.
  2. Ask permission before taking photos of locals.
  3. Carry small change in Euros or Chinese Yuan.

Interesting Facts

The Deep Metro: The Pyongyang Metro is one of the deepest in the world, sitting 100 meters underground so it can double as a nuclear bunker.

The Calendar: North Korea uses the "Juche" calendar, which begins with the birth of Kim Il-sung in 1912 rather than the standard Gregorian year.
