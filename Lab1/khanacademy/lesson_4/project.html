Recipe #1 - Pancakes

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Milk250 ml
Egg1 pc
Flour200 g

Step 1: Mix egg, milk, and sugar.

Step 2: Add flour and fry until golden.

Recipe #2 - Scrambled Eggs

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs3 large
Butter1 tbsp
Salt/Pepperto taste

Step 1: Whisk eggs in a small bowl.

Step 2: Melt butter in a pan and pour in eggs.

Recipe #3 - Fruit Salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Apple2 pcs
Banana1 pc
Honey1 tbsp

Step 1: Chop all fruit into small cubes.

Step 2: Toss in a bowl with honey and serve cold.
